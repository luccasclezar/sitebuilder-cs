To use the theme colors just write the name of the color instead of a Hex or RGB value.

All colors were taken from Material Design Guidelines (except "Background" with Light Theme, that has a slight difference) and the colors "Background", "Card", "TextPrimary", "TextSecondary" and "TextDisabled" are Theme dependant (these three text colors are the same as the black and white variants but change based on the theme).
